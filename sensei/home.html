SELF TAUGHT

I have made use of social media to equip myself with extensive knowledge in graphic design.At the same time, I have mentors who show me the way to follow in my design jouney. Finally, I have done over 20 design projects which have seen me grow into a more competent graphic designer.

IT STUDENT

I am currently pursuing a bachelors degree in Information Technology at The TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY OF KENYA. The school environment has exposed me various influential people and tools in my graphic design journey.

ART ENTHUSIAST

Art is rapidly becoming a big part of my life. Observing and appreciating various forms of art from nature, cars, drawings,paintings,fashion and so on improves my effectiveness as a graphic designer. This is because I therefore make designs that are well appreciated by the consumers.

CLIENT INVOLVEMENT

The design process is split into steps and milestones where you as my client will give approval of the progress or suggest suitable changes before the design process continues.

PUNCTUALITY

I put in effort to ensure the designs are delivered within the agreed timeframe. Besides, I set realistic deadlines for myself to avoid scenarios where mediocre work is done.

PROFESSIONALISM

I leverage all design principles known to me to ensure the final outcome does what it is meant to do. Furthermore, I adhere to legal requirements like copyrights and trademarks when choosing and implementing design tools. Finally, I have transparent terms and conditions which reduce chances of conflicts between clients and myself.

MENTORSHIP

I have active mentors who I always refer to as well as study their work to ensure my designs reach the required market standard and that my skills are growing in the required pace and order.

NEGOTIABILITY

The entire design process from the client reaching out to me, to the negotiation of the price and to the delivery of the final design can be worked out to fit your needs as my client.
