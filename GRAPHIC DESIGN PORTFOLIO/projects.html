FREEDOM

FREEDOM is a poster design that aims to impart a sense of and desire for LIBERATION in every reader's mind.
This has been achieved by use of extensive spacing and colours which invoke a sensation of joy and passion. The focal image used is well known globally as the statue of liberty. This adds visual appeal to the poster as well as amplifying the idea of freedom when glanced at by a viewer.

DREAM

DREAM is a poster which promotes belief in one's ability to achieve milestones that the bigger part of the modern society deems to be beyond human reach.
The image used carries a balance of fiction and reality. This symbolizes that our fantasies are not too far away from what we can acquire. The dominant colour used psychologically arouses an energertic response in the viewer's brain. The energy acquired should be the trigger to begin the journey needed to reach the said milestones.
